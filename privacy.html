Privacy Policy

At Tic Tac Toe Game, we deeply respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any personal information you share with us.

We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) unless you voluntarily provide it (such as player names for in-game display). These names are stored temporarily in your browser (localStorage) and are never shared, sold, or used for any marketing purpose.

Our game may use cookies or session storage to improve gameplay experience, such as saving your scores or game state. These are solely for your convenience and are not used to track or profile you.

We may collect anonymous analytics to understand how users interact with the game. This helps us improve performance and features, but no personal data is included in this process.

As we grow and add new features like online multiplayer or account systems, we’ll update our privacy practices. We’ll always notify users in advance and give full transparency about any changes.

If you have any questions or concerns about privacy, you can reach us at: support@tictactoegame.com
